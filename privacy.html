Stillpoint Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Stillpoint is a private gratitude journal. Your entries stay on your iPhone and sync only through your own iCloud account. We do not operate servers that store your journal.

What we collect

We do not collect personal data on our servers. Journal entries, settings, and weekly reflections are stored on your device and, if you are signed into iCloud, in your private iCloud database associated with your Apple ID.

On-device processing

Patterns and weekly reflections are generated on your device using Apple’s on-device intelligence when available. No journal text is sent to us or to third-party AI services.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, Stillpoint schedules local notifications on your device. No push notification data is sent to us.

Questions about privacy: see the Support page.

Interstill Support

Interstill is a calm, twice-a-day gratitude practice for iPhone (iOS 26+).

Getting started

  • Complete onboarding and set your ritual windows in Settings.
  • Morning: name gratitudes and let one thing go.
  • Evening: name gratitudes and check in on what you set down this morning.

iCloud backup

Sign in to iCloud on your iPhone to sync your journal across your Apple devices. Interstill does not use a separate account.
